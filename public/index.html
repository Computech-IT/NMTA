About New Market Trader's Association

Empowering Commerce, Enriching Community

Foundational Principles

Established as the cornerstone of New Market's commercial ecosystem, the New Market Trader's Association (NMTA) stands as a vanguard institution committed to fostering economic excellence and intergenerational prosperity in Pakyong. As a non-profit organization of repute, we orchestrate synergistic partnerships between established enterprises and emerging entrepreneurs, cultivating a progressive business environment that honors tradition while embracing innovation.

Our Vision

To architect a self-sustaining commercial landscape where heritage commerce and modern entrepreneurship coexist in mutual advancement, ensuring New Market's position as Pakyong's premier business destination.

Our Mission

Preserving Legacy, Building Futures

  • ✓ Policy advocacy & infrastructural modernization
  • ✓ Financial literacy programs & growth capital access
  • ✓ Youth entrepreneurship incubation
  • ✓ Sustainable business practices
  • ✓ Heritage preservation initiatives

Intergenerational Commitment

  • Legacy Fund

"Commerce With Conscience"

Three Pillars of Responsibility

Economic

As a trusted pillar of the local economy, NMTA leverages the strength of its member businesses to influence policy, unlock growth opportunities, and elevate New Market's commercial landscape.

Social

NMTA envisions a thriving business ecosystem where ethical practices and social responsibility go hand in hand to uplift the broader community.

Environmental

Driving sustainable growth through green marketplace initiatives and eco-conscious business practices.

As custodians of New Market's commercial future, NMTA remains unwavering in its commitment to transform individual enterprise into collective prosperity. We invite you to join our movement toward creating an economically vibrant, socially responsible, and intergenerationally sustainable business community.
